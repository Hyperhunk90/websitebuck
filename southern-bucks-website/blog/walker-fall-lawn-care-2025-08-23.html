Walker, LA Fall Lawn Care Checklist

For Bermuda and St. Augustine lawns in Walker, Louisiana:

  • Mowing height: 2.5–3.0 inches going into fall to reduce stress.
  • Pre-emergent: Apply early September for winter weeds (poa annua, chickweed).
  • Overseeding: Optional; focus on weed prevention and soil health first.
  • Fertilization: Low-nitrogen, potassium-support formula before first cool snap.
  • Irrigation: 1 inch/week total including rainfall; reduce as temps drop.
  • Leaf management: Keep turf free of pine straw and oak leaves to avoid fungus.
