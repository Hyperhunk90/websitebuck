Denham Springs Sod & Soil Guide

Before laying sod in Denham Springs, check drainage and soil texture:

  • Drainage: Ensure water evacuates within 24–48 hours after heavy rain.
  • Soil: Clay-heavy? Till in compost/sand blend (3–4 inches) for root penetration.
  • Turf picks: St. Augustine for shade tolerance; Bermuda for sun and traffic.
  • Prep: Grade away from the home, roll after install, water deeply for 2–3 weeks.
  • Edges: Keep edges tight; gaps dry out first and invite weeds.
