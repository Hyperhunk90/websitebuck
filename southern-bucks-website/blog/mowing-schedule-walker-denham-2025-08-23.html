Optimal Mowing Schedule: Walker & Denham Springs

General cadence for warm-season turf in our area:

  • March–April: Every 10–14 days as growth resumes.
  • May–September: Weekly during peak growth and rain.
  • October–November: Every 2 weeks as growth slows.
  • December–February: Monthly or as needed; avoid scalping.
  • Rule of thirds: Never remove more than 1/3 of blade height in one mow.

Prefer set-it-and-forget-it? We offer weekly and bi-weekly plans.
